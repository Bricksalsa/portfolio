Motion Experience

2022
materials Light, projection, interactive systems role Internship — interaction & control systems location Eindhoven

At Motion Experience—an immersive museum focused on light, projection, and spatial storytelling—I worked across several interactive installations. I designed and implemented control systems, created motion and light animations, and experimented with audience-triggered interactions.

The internship deepened how I combine scenography and technology: from VJ software and sensor inputs to timing and dramaturgy of interaction. It was a hands-on way to approach immersive experience design from both an aesthetic and technical angle.
