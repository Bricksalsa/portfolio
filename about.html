about

douwe teusink builds systems that misbehave — interactive installations where spectatorship, framing, and control are staged and then gently sabotaged. with a background in industrial design and a master in expanded scenography, he combines electronics, code, and scenographic setups to turn looking into an event.

recent works include thanks for watching, biecht, and the peepshow series — coin‑ or tap‑activated devices that flip the gaze back to the viewer using cameras and ai‑generated text.
